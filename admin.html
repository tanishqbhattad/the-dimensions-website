Quick Guide

Welcome to your content management panel. Use the sidebar to navigate between sections. Add, edit, or delete content as needed. When you're done, go to Export Data to generate a JSON file. Send that file to your developer (or paste it into your site's data file) to update the live website.

For images: Upload your photos to imgbb.com (free, no signup needed), then paste the direct image URL here. For best results, use images at least 1200px wide.

Export Data

This exports all your content as a JSON file. To update your live website:

  1. Click Download JSON
  2. Rename it to site-data.json
  3. Upload it to your Cloudflare Pages project alongside index.html
  4. Your website will automatically pull the new content
