Who We Are

A Legacy of Design Excellence

Founded in 1996 in Nashik, The Dimensions has grown from a small design studio into one of Maharashtra's most respected architectural firms. With over 25 years of experience, we have shaped skylines and transformed spaces across Nashik, Pune, Mumbai, and beyond.

Our multidisciplinary team of architects, interior designers, and planners work collaboratively to deliver projects that balance beauty with purpose. Every design begins with listening — to our clients, to the site, and to the community it serves.

Our Philosophy

"We believe that great architecture is not about imposing form, but about discovering the natural harmony between space, light, material, and the lives that unfold within. Every project is an opportunity to create something that endures — not just structurally, but emotionally."

— Sachin Bhattad, Principal Architect

The Architect

A Vision Built Over Decades

Sachin Bhattad founded The Dimensions in 1996 in Nashik with a singular vision — to create architecture that stands the test of time while serving the evolving needs of modern life. Trained in classical architectural principles and driven by a passion for innovation, he has led the firm from a small design studio to one of Maharashtra's most respected practices.

His portfolio spans luxury residences, commercial towers, industrial complexes, and institutional buildings across Nashik, Pune, Mumbai, and 10 cities nationwide. Every project under his direction reflects a meticulous attention to detail, a respect for context, and an unwavering commitment to quality craftsmanship.

Beyond design, Sachin is an active contributor to the architectural community, mentoring young architects and advocating for sustainable building practices in India's rapidly urbanising cities.

"Architecture is not just about buildings — it's about crafting the stage on which life unfolds. Every wall, every window, every threshold is an opportunity to elevate the human experience."
— Sachin Bhattad
