Bungalow planning mistakes to avoid in Nashik.

A bungalow is a long-term family asset. Small planning mistakes can affect comfort, privacy, maintenance, and resale value for years.

Common mistakes

  • Ignoring sun path, heat gain, and ventilation.
  • Planning rooms without privacy gradients for guests and family.
  • Leaving parking, services, and storage as afterthoughts.
  • Separating architecture and interiors too late.
  • Not planning future expansion or family changes.

What works better

Start with site orientation, lifestyle mapping, movement flow, light, material durability, and interior needs together. A good bungalow architect in Nashik should balance beauty with daily usability.
