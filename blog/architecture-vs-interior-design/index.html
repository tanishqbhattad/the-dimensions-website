Architecture vs interior design: what do you need?

Many clients in Nashik begin with interiors but later realize layout, services, light, structure, and planning decisions also need architectural thinking.

Architecture handles

  • Building form, layout, structure, site response, drawings, planning, and coordination.
  • Residential, commercial, industrial, and development-level decisions.

Interior design handles

  • Materials, finishes, lighting, furniture, storage, mood, and how the space feels every day.

When integrated design is better

For homes, bungalows, offices, showrooms, and commercial spaces, an integrated architecture and interior design firm can reduce rework because layout and interiors are planned together.
