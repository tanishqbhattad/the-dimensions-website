Sachin Bhattad is the principal architect and founder of The Dimensions, a Nashik-based architecture and interior design firm known for residential, commercial, industrial, and interior projects across Maharashtra.

The Architect

A Nashik practice built over decades.

Sachin Bhattad founded The Dimensions in 1996 with a focus on architecture, interior design, planning, and project execution. The studio's work is rooted in proportion, practicality, material sensitivity, and long-term usability.

His portfolio includes homes, commercial spaces, industrial projects, and refined interiors across Nashik, Pune, Mumbai, and other cities in Maharashtra.

For people searching for an architect in Nashik by name, Sachin Bhattad represents The Dimensions' design leadership, local credibility, and client-first process.
